Our Story

Mark Your Data was founded in 2021 and has evolved from a freelance data scientist into a full-service Data Analytics & AI consultancy firm.

Today, we specialize in three core areas:

Data Strategy

We help organizations define their data vision and create actionable roadmaps to turn data ambitions into reality.

Data Platforms

We build modern, cost-efficient data platforms using open-source tools like DLT and DBT, saving our clients thousands per year.

Data Science & AI

We develop and deploy AI solutions that drive real business value, from predictive models to generative AI applications.
