## How Does a Typical Workshop Look Like?

### Pre-Workshop Preparation (1-2 weeks)
- Stakeholder interviews
- Context gathering
- Material preparation
- Logistics coordination

### Day 1: Explore & Ideate
- **Morning:** Current state review, challenges, and opportunities
- **Afternoon:** Ideation sessions, opportunity refinement

### Day 2: Prioritize & Plan
- **Morning:** Impact assessment, prioritization
- **Afternoon:** Roadmap creation, action planning

### Post-Workshop (1 week)
- Document refinement
- Presentation creation
- Follow-up sessions

**Total Duration:** 1-2 day workshop + 2-3 weeks prep/follow-up

---

## Workshop Format Options

### Full Team Offsite (Recommended)
- 1-2 days, off-site location
- 8-20 participants
- Deep dive with breaks and team building
- Best for major initiatives

### Executive Strategy Session
- Half-day to full-day
- C-level and senior leadership
- High-level strategy and priorities
- Best for setting direction

### Virtual Workshop Series
- 3-4 half-day sessions
- Remote participation
- Spread over 1-2 weeks
- Best for distributed teams
