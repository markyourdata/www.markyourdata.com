### Who Is This For?

This service is designed for **teams new to data & AI** who need guidance to get started:

- **Organizations without data expertise** - You have data but lack the skills to extract value from it
- **Teams feeling overwhelmed** - You know data could help but don't know where to start or what's possible
- **Businesses with scattered data** - You have systems collecting data but no one connecting the dots
- **Leaders seeking validation** - You want to understand what's realistic before making bigger investments

If you're at the beginning of your data journey and need a trusted guide to help you take those first steps, this service is for you.

### Why Start with Data Discovery?

Jumping directly into data projects without understanding your landscape often leads to wasted effort and disappointment. Common pitfalls we help you avoid:

- **Building the wrong thing** - Investing in solutions that don't address real business needs
- **Underestimating complexity** - Data quality issues and integration challenges derail projects
- **Missing quick wins** - Overlooking simple opportunities that could deliver value quickly
- **Team frustration** - Lack of foundational knowledge leads to confusion and resistance

Data discovery provides a safe, guided environment to learn what's possible with your data, understand limitations, and identify realistic opportunities before committing to larger initiatives.

### How We Guide Your Discovery

We work hands-on with your team for 3 month to explore your data landscape:

**1. Data Landscape Mapping** - Together, we inventory your data sources, understand what data you have, where it lives, and how it flows through your organization.

**2. Use Case Exploration** - We facilitate workshops with your team to identify business questions that data could help answer and evaluate what's feasible.

**3. Hands-On Analysis** - We work alongside your team to explore promising opportunities, demonstrating analytical techniques while building your team's skills.

**4. Experiment setup** - Based on what we discover, we help you setup an experiment to proof if the value is really there. This will help you decide wether to invest more and scale up. Or to move resources to a next discovery.

Throughout the process, we transfer knowledge so your team develops the capability to continue the data journey independently.
