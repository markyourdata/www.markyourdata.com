Data & AI leadership

We embed in your organization to build your data capability from the ground up, until you're ready to lead independently

What we do

Need data leadership but not ready to hire a full-time lead? We join your organization to guide strategy, platform, and execution. One of our senior consultants becomes your interim data leader.

How it works

Three phases that take you from strategy to independence.

Phase 1

Strategic foundation

Build your data strategy and roadmap

  • Develop use cases and business cases
  • Create a data & AI roadmap aligned with business goals
  • Define success metrics and KPIs
  • Identify high-value opportunities
Phase 2

Platform setup

Build the technical foundation

  • Design and implement data infrastructure
  • Establish data governance frameworks
  • Build scalable technical foundations
  • Create data pipelines and models
Phase 3

Team building & execution

Execute and build internal capability

  • Set up and structure data teams
  • Lead execution of initial use cases
  • Coach and develop team capabilities
  • Establish best practices and ways of working

Our commitment

We stay on board until your team has successfully delivered one or more use cases AND you've hired or trained the right person to take over. You focus on finding the right long-term fit while we handle everything else.
