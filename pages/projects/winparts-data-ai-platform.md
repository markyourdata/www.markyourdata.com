### Impact on the Organization

The platform transformed how Winparts worked with data. Analysts could now access clean, modeled data instead of wrestling with raw sources. Time previously spent on data preparation shifted to actual analysis and insight generation.

Most importantly, the infrastructure created a foundation for AI initiatives. With reliable data pipelines and quality data models, the team could confidently move forward with machine learning projects knowing the data foundation was solid.
