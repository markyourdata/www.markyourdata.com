### How we work: our methodology

We follow our proven **Make it Work → Make it Better → Make it Scale** methodology, adapting the approach based on whether you're discovering new opportunities or validating a specific use case:

**Discovery Track (3 months):** For strategic objectives without a clear use case yet

<div class="process-steps">

<div class="process-step">
<div class="step-number">1</div>
<img src="../../../assets/icons/regular/light-bulb.svg" alt="Ideation" class="step-icon" />
<h4>Strategic Objective & Ideation</h4>
<p>You provide a strategic objective. We facilitate ideation with your team and launch broad exploratory analyses across multiple directions—going wide to find real opportunities.</p>
</div>

<div class="process-step">
<div class="step-number">2</div>
<img src="../../../assets/icons/regular/search.svg" alt="Deep Dive" class="step-icon" />
<h4>Deep Dive Analysis</h4>
<p>Based on exploration results, we select 1-3 promising directions for deeper analysis. We work alongside your team to uncover which specific levers drive the most business impact.</p>
</div>

<div class="process-step">
<div class="step-number">3</div>
<img src="../../../assets/icons/regular/flask.svg" alt="Experiment" class="step-icon" />
<h4>Experiment Setup</h4>
<p>When we've confirmed which buttons to push, we design and launch an experiment to prove the value. The experiment runs beyond 3 months to gather sufficient data for solid conclusions.</p>
</div>

</div>

**Proof of Value Track (10-12 weeks):** For validating a specific, defined use case before full investment

<div class="process-steps">

<div class="process-step">
<div class="step-number">1</div>
<img src="../../../assets/icons/regular/flask.svg" alt="Make it Work" class="step-icon" />
<h4>Make it Work</h4>
<p>Build working proof of concept with production data. Implement core functionality and key algorithms. Create basic user interface for stakeholder testing.</p>
</div>

<div class="process-step">
<div class="step-number">2</div>
<img src="../../../assets/icons/regular/graph-up.svg" alt="Make it Better" class="step-icon" />
<h4>Make it Better</h4>
<p>Run pilot with real users in controlled environment. Measure actual business impact versus projections. Gather user feedback and identify improvements.</p>
</div>

<div class="process-step">
<div class="step-number">3</div>
<img src="../../../assets/icons/regular/check-circle.svg" alt="Make it Scale" class="step-icon" />
<h4>Make it Scale</h4>
<p>Evaluate infrastructure and integration requirements. Estimate full implementation timeline and cost. Calculate validated ROI based on pilot results.</p>
</div>

<div class="process-step">
<div class="step-number">4</div>
<img src="../../../assets/icons/regular/trophy.svg" alt="Decision" class="step-icon" />
<h4>Go/No-Go</h4>
<p>Present findings with data-backed recommendation. If "Go"—deliver production roadmap. If "No-Go"—save significant investment and pivot.</p>
</div>

</div>

**Throughout both tracks:** Regular insight sessions where we share results and decide next steps together. We transfer knowledge continuously so your team builds capability.

### When: ideal timing to start

**One discovery per quarter is the ideal pace.** This rhythm allows you to:

- Launch a new discovery each quarter aligned with quarterly planning cycles
- Give experiments from previous quarters time to run and generate conclusions
- Build momentum with continuous validated learning
- Maintain focus rather than spreading analytical resources too thin

Common trigger points to start your first discovery:
- Beginning of fiscal or calendar year when setting annual priorities
- After strategic planning sessions that identified data & AI opportunities
- When leadership asks "how can we use data to achieve X goal?" and no one has a clear answer
- Before making major investments in data platforms or hiring—prove the use case first
