### How We Work: Our Methodology

We guide you through a structured 5-6 week process that moves from strategic thinking to actionable roadmap:

<div class="process-steps">

<div class="process-step">
<div class="step-number">1</div>
<img src="../../../assets/icons/regular/strategy.svg" alt="Strategic Alignment" class="step-icon" />
<h4>Strategic Alignment</h4>
<p>We understand your business challenges, strategic goals, and current data capabilities. Through stakeholder interviews, we identify key areas where data & AI can drive value.</p>
</div>

<div class="process-step">
<div class="step-number">2</div>
<img src="../../../assets/icons/regular/light-bulb.svg" alt="Use Case Discovery" class="step-icon" />
<h4>Use Case Discovery</h4>
<p>Facilitated workshops with cross-functional teams to identify pain points, explore solutions, define success metrics, and assess data requirements. We uncover 10-20 potential use cases.</p>
</div>

<div class="process-step">
<div class="step-number">3</div>
<img src="../../../assets/icons/regular/priority-high.svg" alt="Evaluation" class="step-icon" />
<h4>Evaluation & Prioritization</h4>
<p>We score and rank each opportunity using our framework: business impact, technical feasibility, data readiness, effort, and strategic fit. This creates an objective investment basis.</p>
</div>

<div class="process-step">
<div class="step-number">4</div>
<img src="../../../assets/icons/regular/map.svg" alt="Roadmap" class="step-icon" />
<h4>Roadmap Development</h4>
<p>Deliver a comprehensive roadmap with prioritized use case portfolio, implementation timeline, resource requirements, and expected outcomes with ROI projections.</p>
</div>
